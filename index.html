18. Semiautomatic Crop Farm

This farm is semi-automatic which means we just have to plant the seeds of whatever crop you need then when its time to harvest just push the lever, crop will be harvested and will move to chest. It's because crop can't be planted under light level, so they just harvest on their own once we harvest one of them.
